High buildability + AI-resistant (best place to start): Robotics (7), Cybersecurity (8), Care-tools (21), Hardware (25), most of Tier C (17вЂ“20). Ship now and durable.
High buildability + AI-pressured (build to learn, don't over-commit): Applied AI (1), Fintech (5), Edtech (6), Creator (15), Design (18). Easiest demos, hardest long-term moat.
